How do you write these fics of yours? Like the idea, planning, editing, character development, etc (whatever you want)? Especially the longer one, cause wow!! I love the development of your stories so much and can see the thought and effort you put into it!!

shamefully, i don't plan my fics at all. no outline, i just begin with vibes and develop the plot/overall theme/conflict out of the scenes that i write down. i will go back and edit scenes so they fit to the common thread running through the fic if i have to. that's why my non-pwp fics usually have no 'trope' plots and are more of a slice of life, character study vibe, centered on a character who has issues or something that needs to be worked out between the central characters. my lack of outlining or preplanning is kind of the reason i have 5x more wips than i do published fics (and i have 40 published fics), but outlining has never really worked for me so it is what it is.

typically, the ideas for a fic comes from moments i see between members or something they said or a dynamic between them i'm interested in exploring. so the jayke i posted recently, i wanted to explore the dynamic of how you could argue jake is everyone's 'favorite' (and converted that into them all having feelings for him) but how that can be harmful. also his recent interview about being guarded inspired the character introspection side of it and the explanation of why he behaves that way. and then some of the scenes i included were directly inspired from jayke moments at the beginning of this year and also jay's agenda for puppy jake; hence the puppy play. i just get struck by a concept and want to write it.

character development usually centers around the character i give issues cause they're the pov of the story. sometimes i try to think from the character's pov, like what would they do in this situation at their current mental/emotional/physical state and i find that works for me? other times it's like okay, i wrote a beginning scene where character A is starting at this point mentally, emotionally, and relationship wise, and then i've written this later scene where character A has now moved to this point mentally, emotionally, and relationship wise. so what are the steps in between that need to be taken to move them there. but i will also say, most of this is subconscious. i don't really actively think how should i develop this character, i follow instinct. i think we subconsciously understand how people work, think, and act better than we might expect, so i just trust where my brain is taking me.

i'm also not a very consistent writer, i write in fits and bursts. i won't touch a fic for a month and then i'll write half of it in three days. but in a way that space between writing sessions means i can review my fic with fresh eyes and pick out anything that doesn't make sense or isn't explained enough or completely unnecessary.

but yeah the development of my fics is usually a lot of me trusting my instincts, asking myself does this make sense for the characters to do, and trying to get characters from point A to point B.
